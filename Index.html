La_Tarvenah Cafe

Appetizers

  1. Caprese Salad - Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, basil, and balsamic glaze.
  2. Spring Rolls - Crispy vegetable rolls served with a sweet and sour dipping sauce.
  3. Bruschetta - Toasted bread topped with a mixture of diced tomatoes, basil, garlic, and olive oil.

Main Courses

  1. Grilled Salmon - Fresh salmon fillet grilled to perfection and served with a side of vegetables.
  2. Beef Stir-Fry - Tender strips of beef sautéed with vegetables in a savory sauce, served over rice.
  3. BBQ Ribs - Slow-cooked pork ribs glazed with tangy barbecue sauce, served with coleslaw and cornbread.

Desserts

  1. Molten Chocolate Lava Cake - Rich chocolate cake with a gooey molten center, served with vanilla ice cream.
  2. Apple Pie - Classic apple pie with a flaky crust, served warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  3. Crème Brûlée - Silky custard topped with a caramelized sugar crust, perfect for a sweet ending to your meal.
